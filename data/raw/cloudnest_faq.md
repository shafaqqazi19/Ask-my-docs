# CloudNest FAQ

## Account & Access

**How do I reset my password?**
Use the "Forgot password" link on the login page to receive a reset email. The
reset link expires after 60 minutes. If you no longer have access to your
registered email, contact support to verify your identity manually.

**Can I change my workspace's URL slug?**
Yes, from Settings > Workspace. Note that changing the slug breaks any previously
bookmarked or shared links to your workspace.

## Billing & Cancellation

**How do I cancel my subscription?**
You can cancel at any time from Account Settings > Billing. Access continues
until the end of the current billing period. No partial refunds are issued for
unused time in the current cycle, except where required by local law.

**Are annual plans refundable?**
Annual plans can be refunded in full within 14 days of purchase if unused.
Monthly plans are non-refundable but can be cancelled anytime.

**Do you offer discounts for non-profits?**
Yes, registered non-profits receive 20% off Pro and Enterprise annual plans.
Contact billing@cloudnest.example with proof of non-profit status.

## Files & Storage

**What is the maximum file upload size?**
25 MB per file on the Free plan, and 5 GB per file on Pro and Enterprise plans.

**How long are deleted files recoverable?**
Deleted files move to Trash and are recoverable for 30 days before permanent
deletion.

## Support

**What are the support response times?**
Free tier support is community forum only with no guaranteed response time. Pro
plan support responds within 4 business hours. Enterprise support responds
within 1 hour, 24/7.

**Is two-factor authentication required?**
Two-factor authentication (2FA) is optional but strongly recommended for Free
and Pro workspaces, and mandatory for all Enterprise workspace admins.
